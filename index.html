TomTom GO Navigation Map Update

The TomTom GO Navigation series offers advanced routing and real-time traffic updates for seamless driving experiences. Regular map updates are essential to keep your device current with new roads, updated POIs, and accurate directions.

Why Update Your TomTom GO Maps?

  • Access the latest roads and intersections
  • Correct map errors and outdated routes
  • Find updated gas stations, restaurants, and POIs
  • Improve navigation accuracy and avoid detours

Supported GO Devices

  • TomTom GO 620, GO 520, GO 5200, GO 6200
  • TomTom GO Basic, GO Essential
  • TomTom GO Discover, GO Exclusive
  • TomTom GO Premium and others with MyDrive

How to Update TomTom GO Maps

  • Install MyDrive Connect from the TomTom website
  • Connect your GO device via USB to your computer
  • Open MyDrive Connect and log in to your TomTom account
  • Select your device and choose "Update Now"
  • Let the update complete before unplugging the device

Wi-Fi Update (for compatible models)

  • Connect your GO device to a Wi-Fi network
  • Go to Settings > Updates > Check for Updates
  • Select available updates and install them directly

Important Tips

  • Ensure the device is fully charged or plugged in
  • Use a stable internet connection
  • Remove unused maps to free up memory space

Updating your TomTom GO device is key to enjoying accurate and smooth navigation. Whether you're heading across town or across the country, updated maps help you reach your destination efficiently.
